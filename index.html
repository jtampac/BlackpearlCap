The Firm

A multi-family office, evolved into a co-investment platform.

Black Pearl Capital was established as a multi-family office to manage the proprietary investments of its founding partners. It has since evolved into a co-investment platform specialising in opportunistic private and public equity across industry sectors.

Headquartered in Geneva with offices across four jurisdictions, the firm invests globally — spanning the United States, Europe, Asia and the Middle East — with a preference for control positions and patient, long-term ownership.

Our global network is the engine of the firm — identifying opportunities, allocating capital efficiently, executing swiftly and nurturing the management teams that steward it. Black Pearl Capital

Investment Philosophy

Capital deployed with conviction, preserved with discipline.

Proprietary Investments

A prudent, opportunistic stance across global markets — seeking value while weighing downside risk. We favour control positions that allow active collaboration with management, sizing each commitment carefully and holding without a predefined investment horizon.

Co-Investment Platform

Black Pearl Capital Partners SPC, a Cayman-domiciled private fund regulated by CIMA, enables co-investors to participate alongside the firm — in a single transaction or across portfolios that express our highest-conviction strategies, under independent oversight.

Capital Preservation

As conservative investors we avoid leveraging our positions and size each commitment to account for downside risk.

Long-Term Value

We monitor market conditions and business cycles to compound value patiently, unconstrained by a conventional horizon.

Operational Excellence

We work alongside management to develop and execute strategy, contributing both capital and on-the-ground support.

Risk Management

Independent administration, depository, legal and audit counterparties underpin robust governance for every mandate.

Leadership

Our founders.

Co-Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Reza Irani-Kermani

A career spanning the world’s bullion and currency markets, brought to bear on long-term principal investment.

A financial executive educated in France and the United Kingdom, Reza graduated from the University of Birmingham in 1989 and began his career trading bullion and foreign exchange at Management Investment and Trade (MIT) in London, a subsidiary of MKS Finance.

By 1991 he had risen to General Manager, leading MIT through substantial growth and personally overseeing daily turnover in excess of $10 billion. During his tenure he managed the strategic transition surrounding Po Sang Bank — a sister bank to the Bank of China — and orchestrated the subsequent profitable sale of its stake to Amalgamated Metals Corporation. Prior to co-founding Black Pearl Capital, he was an executive at The Abela Corporation.

Co-Founder & Chief Operating Officer

Abbas Jafarian

A chartered financier and operator who has structured capital across Europe and the MENA region.

Previously a co-founder of Symphony Partners Plc, a property and asset advisory firm, Abbas identified and structured over €700 million of real estate and private equity transactions across the Eurozone, and served on the investment committees of several MENA-based investment trusts. Earlier roles include investment executive at Morgan Stanley and chartered accountant at Deloitte and Arthur Andersen.

He qualified as a chartered accountant with Deloitte in 1992 and holds an MBA from London Business School (1998). A noted sportsman and environmental advocate, in 2017 he became the first Iranian to trek across Antarctica to the South Pole — a journey documented by the Discovery Channel.
